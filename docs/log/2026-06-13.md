## Finding: TB2-LOCAL-001 — gemma4-12b not viable for Terminal-Bench 2.0
- terminus-2 + gemma4-12b through the litellm proxy, full TB2 (89 trials,
  -n 1 serial), 1800s/trial agent cap.
- **17/17 completed trials errored, all AgentTimeoutError** (timed out
  after 1800s mid-LLM-call). Not a routing failure — single gemma4-12b
  proxy calls are fast. CUMULATIVE: TB2 tasks need many agent turns over
  long terminal-output contexts; a local 12B on the 3080 Ti cannot finish
  a task inside 30 min, so every trial exhausts its budget -> 0 reward.
- Decision: KILLED task 112 (would have been ~tens of GPU-hours serial for
  a ~0% all-timeout result). Lingering trial container (dtbpzmm) removed;
  unrelated path-tracing__ppdyeij (Up 25h) left untouched.
- Implication: viable local TB2 baselines need either a much higher
  per-trial cap (GPU-days, dubious value) or a faster/smaller-context
  approach. Frontier-via-cloud-lane is the realistic TB2 anchor; local
  agentic capability is better measured on the lab's own shorter suites
  (hard/brutal), where the FT 4B just showed real gains.
